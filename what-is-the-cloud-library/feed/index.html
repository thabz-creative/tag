Hi! Thank you for reaching out to us. If you disable tagDiv Cloud Library, the templates won’t be accessible and available anymore on your site. If you have built your site using Cloud Templates and you deactivate the plugin, instead of the template you’ll see a list of shortcodes on the frontend. Please note that the tagDiv Cloud Library does not store all the templates in your theme files – they are stored in cloud. The only cloud templates available in your theme files are the ones you have installed. Also, the website’s speed is influenced by other factors, including hosting, optimized content, other installed plugins, incompatibility between plugins, and more.
